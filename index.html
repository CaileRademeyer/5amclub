About GameFit 5am Club

We are a mobile rugby and fitness training squad that travels to schools, clubs and teams across the region. Our sessions are designed for players from the ages of under 7s upto under 18s mastering techniques and abilities, with an emphasis of hardwork, fun, safety, and measurable progress.

What We Offer

Rugby Coaching

Structured rugby training delivered by experienced coaches. We focus on skill development, game awareness, set-pieces and team play.

Fitness Conditioning

High-energy fitness drills that improve strength, speed, agility and endurance. Great for preseason preparation or in-season support.

Team Workshops

Custom workshops for teams and schools, including warm-up routines, injury prevention, and warm-down routines making sure players do not get injured.

Sample Drills

  • Agility Ladder: Improve footwork and reaction time with dynamic ladder sequences.
  • Conditioning Circuits: Rotate through strength, cardio and plyometric stations for total-body fitness.
  • Tackle Technique: Safe and effective contact drills to build confidence in defence.
  • Team Relay Challenges: Boost teamwork and competitive spirit with relay-style drills.
